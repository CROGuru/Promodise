What is Conversion Rate Optimisation (CRO)?

CRO is a system for increasing the percentage of visitors to a website that convert into customers.
It is a strategy which maps out the entire customer journey, detailing each element to ensure it's being comprehensive measured and optimised to ultimately increase revenue.

Irrespective of the focus of your business, an interactive, smooth, and coherent online platform is essential for being competitive in 2021. Understand the most important part of your business, and turn website visitors into happy customers.

Why should you take advantage of CRO?

The Optimised website has an average Conversion Rate of 5.6%. This is over a 200% increase in Conversions, which can equate to a 200% increase in business revenue.
