Why is CRO so important?

The average conversion funnel loses 76% of potential customers along the way. Globally, this value represented 4.6 trillion dollars of lost revenue. CRO enhances your website to improve the customer journey, the more optimisation that takes place the higher the conversion of the website.

  • Customer Journey

    CRO is not just about attracting more customers to your website, but about improving the customer journal through the conversion funnel. This ensures customers with purchasing intent can navigate and complete checkout efficiently.

  • Customer Focused

    CRO ensures that it is easier and simpler for potential and existing customers to take action within your website. An approach that keeps customers are the core of the strategy will implement changes that customers will appreciate. This turns website traffic into happy customers.

  • CRO Cycle

    CRO involves improved functionality, optimisation, customer satisfaction, and an increase in perceived product value. Understanding what appeals to your audience is essential in staying ahead of the competition. Continuous improvement will ensure this competitive advantage is maintained and translates to business growth.

  • Detail Orientated

    Improve on all elements of the conversion funnel; proposition, clarity, relevance, transparency, distraction and sense of urgency. If you evaluate each of these factors properly, you’ll come away with a wholistic collection of insights that will lead you to success.
